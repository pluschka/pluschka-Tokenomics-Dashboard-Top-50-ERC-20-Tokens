Authoritative Sources

Glossary:
Smart Contract Audits -	Independent code reviews to detect and fix vulnerabilities in smart contracts.
Bug Bounty Programs - Reward programs encouraging discovery and reporting of security flaws.
Multi-Signature Wallets - Wallets requiring multiple signatures for transaction authorization.
Insurance Funds - Funds reserved to compensate users in case of protocol failure or hacks.
Decentralized Governance - Community voting ensures decentralized decision-making and reduces single-point risk.
Rate Limiting & Circuit Breakers - Mechanisms to halt abnormal activity, protecting from exploits or price crashes.
Token Vesting & Lockups - Gradual release of tokens to prevent market manipulation or rug pulls.
Oracle Security - Securing data feeds to prevent manipulation of price or off-chain information.
Slashing Mechanisms - Penalties for malicious or dishonest validator behavior in staking protocols.
KYC/AML Compliance - Identity checks to reduce fraud and comply with regulations.
Other - If none of the above applies.
